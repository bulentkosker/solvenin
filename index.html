✦ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the free plan really free forever?
+
Yes. No credit card required. Our free plan includes 1 user, 1 module, and up to 100 records per month — forever. Upgrade only when you need more.
Can I use Solvenin in my language?
+
Yes. Solvenin supports 10+ languages including English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, Arabic, Chinese, Russian, Portuguese, and Japanese — with more coming soon.
How does per-user pricing work?
+
You pay only for active users. If you have 3 users on the Professional plan, you pay $9 × 3 = $27/month. You can add or remove users anytime.
Can I switch between plans?
+
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle.
Is my data secure?
+
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Each company's data is fully isolated. We use enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime SLA.
