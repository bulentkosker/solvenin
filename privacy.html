Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

We collect only what we need to run the service. We never sell your data. You own your data and can export or delete it anytime.

This Privacy Policy explains how Solvenin collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our cloud-based ERP platform at solvenin.com.

1. Information We Collect

Account information including name, email address, and company name when you register. Business data you enter into Solvenin such as customers, products, and invoices. Usage and technical data including IP address and browser type. Payment processing is handled by Paddle — we never store card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send important service updates
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud
  • To improve our Service based on usage

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS/SSL in transit. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure each company's data is completely isolated from other users.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We share data only with essential providers: Supabase (database), Paddle (payments), and Vercel (hosting). All providers are bound by strict data processing agreements.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and export your data at any time
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Transfer your data to another service

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. After cancellation, data is available for export for 30 days, then permanently deleted.

8. GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us at [email protected] to exercise your rights.

9. Children

Our Service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
