Refund Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you are not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund — no questions asked.

1. Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to any paid plan and are not satisfied for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial payment. This applies to first-time subscriptions only.

2. Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request it within 14 days of your first payment
  • It is your first paid subscription on that account
  • Your account is in good standing with no Terms of Service violations

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Renewal charges after the initial 14-day period
  • Accounts that have violated our Terms of Service
  • Partial month refunds after the 14-day window
  • Downgrades from a higher plan to a lower plan

4. How to Request a Refund

Contact us at support@solvenin.com with:

  • Your account email address
  • The reason for your refund request (optional)
  • Your Paddle transaction ID (found in your payment receipt)

We will process your refund within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

5. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. After cancellation, you retain access until the end of your current billing period. Cancellation does not automatically trigger a refund — you must request one within the 14-day window.

6. Exceptional Circumstances

If you were charged due to a technical error or unauthorized access outside the 14-day window, please contact us and we will review your case individually.

If you are experiencing issues with the Service, please contact our support team first — we may be able to resolve your problem before a refund is necessary.
